Privacy Policy

Last Updated: May 10, 2026

1. Introduction

FretTracks ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use the FretTracks mobile application ("App"). This policy complies with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR), the UK General Data Protection Regulation (UK GDPR), the Australian Privacy Act 1988, and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA).

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Account Information

  • Email Address (Optional): Used solely for account management and service-related communications.
  • User ID: A unique identifier for managing your subscription and app preferences.

2.2 Usage Analytics

  • App Usage Data: Anonymous statistics about features, backing tracks, and scales you access to improve our service.
  • Performance Data: Anonymous technical data (e.g., crash reports) to maintain and improve the App.
  • Device Information: Basic device details (iOS version, device model) for compatibility and support.

2.3 Subscription Information

  • Purchase Data: Subscription status and purchase information processed through Apple's App Store. We do not store payment card information.

3. Legal Basis for Processing (EU/UK Users)

We process your personal data only where we have a valid legal basis. The legal bases we rely on are:

  • Contract Performance: Processing your account information and subscription data is necessary to provide the App and its features to you.
  • Legitimate Interests: We process anonymous usage and performance data to improve the App, fix technical issues, and develop new features. This processing does not override your privacy rights.
  • Consent: Where you have provided explicit consent, we may send you marketing and promotional emails. You may withdraw consent at any time.
  • Legal Obligation: We may process your data where required by applicable law.

4. How We Use Your Information

4.1 Service Provision

  • Manage your account and subscription
  • Provide access to App features and content
  • Sync your preferences across devices

4.2 Communication

  • Send account-related notifications (subscription status, renewals, etc.)
  • Provide customer support when requested
  • Send service updates and important App information

4.3 App Improvement

  • Analyze anonymous usage patterns to improve content and features
  • Monitor app performance and fix technical issues
  • Develop new features based on user preferences

5. Email Communications and Consent

5.1 Types of Emails

With your consent, we may send you:

  • Account and subscription management emails
  • App updates and new feature announcements
  • Educational content related to guitar learning
  • Customer support responses

5.2 Your Consent and Rights

  • Opt-in Required: We will only send marketing emails with your explicit consent.
  • Easy Unsubscribe: Every email includes an unsubscribe link.
  • Account Emails: Some transactional emails (e.g., subscription confirmations) are necessary for service operation.
  • Your Choice: You can opt out of promotional emails while still receiving essential account communications.

5.3 CAN-SPAM Compliance

We comply with the CAN-SPAM Act by:

  • Never using false or misleading header information
  • Not using deceptive subject lines
  • Clearly identifying promotional messages
  • Including our physical address in emails
  • Providing easy unsubscribe mechanisms
  • Honoring opt-out requests promptly

6. Information Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may collect information:

  • Apple App Store: Processes subscription payments and provides purchase data.
  • Firebase (Google): Provides authentication, analytics, and app infrastructure.
  • RevenueCat: Manages subscription status and billing.

6.2 No Data Sales

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or to protect our rights and the safety of our users.

7. International Data Transfers

FretTracks is based in the United States. If you use the App from the EU, UK, Australia, or other regions, your information may be transferred to, stored, and processed in the United States.

For EU and UK users, we ensure such transfers are protected through appropriate safeguards, including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs) approved by the European Commission and, where applicable, the UK International Data Transfer Agreement (IDTA). Our third-party service providers (Google Firebase, RevenueCat, Apple) maintain their own appropriate transfer mechanisms.

For Australian users, personal information transferred overseas is handled in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles. We take reasonable steps to ensure overseas recipients handle your information in a manner consistent with those principles.

8. Data Storage and Security

8.1 No User-Generated Content Storage

We do not store any personal content, files, or user-generated data on our servers.

8.2 Data Security

  • All data transmission is encrypted using industry-standard protocols.
  • We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information.
  • Access to personal data is restricted to authorized personnel only.

8.3 Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained while your account is active and deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • Analytics Data: Anonymous usage data may be retained for up to 3 years for service improvement.
  • Email Data: Email addresses are deleted within 30 days of an account deletion request.
  • Subscription Data: Retained for 7 years where required for financial and legal compliance.

9. Your Privacy Rights

9.1 All Users

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we have about you
  • Correct inaccurate personal information
  • Request deletion of your account and associated data
  • Opt out of promotional emails
  • Data portability (where technically feasible)

9.2 EU and UK Users (GDPR / UK GDPR)

In addition to the above, EU and UK residents have the right to:

  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your personal data where we rely on legitimate interests as our legal basis.
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Request that we restrict processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Where processing is based on consent, withdraw that consent at any time without affecting the lawfulness of prior processing.
  • Right to Lodge a Complaint: You have the right to lodge a complaint with your local data protection supervisory authority. In the EU, this is your national data protection authority. In the UK, this is the Information Commissioner's Office (ICO) at ico.org.uk.

We will respond to all rights requests within 30 days of receipt.

9.3 Australian Users (Privacy Act 1988)

Australian residents have rights under the Privacy Act 1988 and the Australian Privacy Principles, including the right to access and correct personal information we hold about you. If you believe we have not handled your personal information in accordance with the Australian Privacy Principles, you may contact us at support@frettracks.com. If you are not satisfied with our response, you may lodge a complaint with the Office of the Australian Information Commissioner (OAIC) at oaic.gov.au.

9.4 California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

California residents have additional rights, including:

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell personal information)
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights

9.5 How to Exercise Your Rights

Contact us at support@frettracks.com with your request. We will respond within 30 days.

10. Children's Privacy

FretTracks is not intended for children under 13 (or under 16 in the EU/UK where applicable). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children below these ages. If we discover such information has been collected, we will delete it immediately.
