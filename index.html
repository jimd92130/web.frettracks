Learn scales. Play music.

Fret Freedom bridges the gap between learning scales and using them in real songs. Pick a scale or mode, hear it, see the tab, and jam over chord progressions with high‑quality backing tracks.

Focus: Ionian, Dorian, Phrygian, Lydian, Mixolydian, Aeolian, Locrian, plus Major/Minor Pentatonic and Blues.

What you can do

Understand the scale

Clear scale characteristics and tabs so you know what makes each mode unique.

Play over real chords

Chord progressions and vamps that highlight the unique sound of each scale.

Practice with tracks

Quality backing tracks in multiple keys and tempos that loop smoothly.

Stay practical

Straightforward guidance in plain language. No fluff. Learn fast and have fun.
